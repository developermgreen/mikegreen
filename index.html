About me

       I am a student at Hewlett High School interested in computer science. My interest began Freshman year after tkaing weekend classes and an intro to programming class. After my freshman year I attended the MakeSchool iOS bootcamp. In the 8 week boot camp I learned swift and developed my first app using firebase - Just Service! During my sophomore year I also interned at a start-up by developing the front end of the iOS app Skinno, which I no longer work on. I also have taken many online courses.

       During my time coding I haven't only developed, but I have also taught and gave back. After my sophomore year I took an 8 week web design and development bootcamp. At libraries I hold free coding workshops for teens in middle school and high school. Not everyone is able to attend bootcamps or may be unsure about coding, so I hope that this course will extend an opportunity to everyone. I have also taught at the Upperline School of Code. I hope to possibly inspire my students to explore this rising sector.
